Data Deletion Policy

This page explains how to delete your Pill Scan account and all associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

1 Open the Pill Scan app on your device

2 Go to Settings (gear icon)

3 Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"

4 Read the warning message carefully

5 Type DELETE to confirm

6 Tap "Delete My Account" button

Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted.

What Data is Deleted?

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Your email address and authentication credentials
  • Medicine Records: All medicines you have added, including names, quantities, and expiration dates
  • Labels: All custom labels you have created
  • Medication Schedules: All scheduled medication reminders
  • Adherence History: All records of medications taken or skipped
  • Barcodes: Any scanned barcode data associated with your medicines

Data Retention

After account deletion:

  • All personal data is deleted immediately from our active databases
  • Backup data is purged within 30 days
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained (contains no personal information)

Alternative: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion via email:

Send an email to: mezidosoft@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 business days.

Before You Delete

Consider exporting your data first!

Before deleting your account, you can export your medicine data as PDF or CSV:

  1. Go to Settings > Export Data
  2. Choose PDF or CSV format
  3. Save or share the exported file
