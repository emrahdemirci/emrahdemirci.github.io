SnaxAI - Check Foods for Diet Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data may be processed when you use the SnaxAI - Check Foods for Diet application, how that data is used, and what rights you may have.

SnaxAI - Check Foods for Diet is a mobile application that helps analyze products using barcodes, ingredient text, and user preferences. The app may include camera access, photo selection, OCR, subscription management, and server-based analysis features.

1. Information We Collect

SnaxAI - Check Foods for Diet may process the following categories of data:

  • Camera data: The app may request camera access to scan barcodes or capture product ingredient information.
  • Photos and images: If you choose or capture an image, that image may be used to extract ingredient text and support analysis.
  • Barcode data: Barcodes that you scan or enter manually may be processed.
  • Text and ingredient content: Ingredient lists you enter manually or text extracted through OCR may be processed.
  • Product information: Product name, brand, ingredient list, source URL, and related product metadata may be processed.
  • Usage and device context data: The app may process a unique installation ID, language preference, plan status, and similar technical or usage context data.
  • Subscription data: For in-app purchases and subscription management, subscription status, package information, and customer status may be processed through RevenueCat and the relevant app store infrastructure.
  • History and preference data: Some data, such as analysis history, onboarding state, and language preferences, may be stored on your device.

2. How We Use Information

We may use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To resolve barcodes and match products
  • To extract text from photos or camera input using OCR
  • To generate ingredient analysis and compatibility assessments
  • To manage usage limits, plan status, and subscription status
  • To personalize the app experience, including language preferences
  • To maintain app performance, security, and service continuity
  • To show analysis history and related in-app screens

3. Camera and Photo Access

The app uses camera permission only to support features such as barcode scanning and capturing ingredient information from product packaging.

  • The camera is activated only when you choose to use a camera-based feature.
  • Photo selection or capture happens only as a result of your direct action.
  • If you do not grant camera access, you may still be able to use some alternative flows, such as manual input, but camera-based features may not work.

4. How Information Is Shared

SnaxAI - Check Foods for Diet may share data with the following parties in order to provide the service:

  • Backend service providers: Data may be exchanged with the app's backend services for barcode lookup, ingredient extraction, analysis, and history or service-related features.
  • Third-party subscription and purchase infrastructure: RevenueCat and the relevant app store providers may be used for subscription management.
  • External product or data sources: External databases or web sources may be used for barcode resolution or product information matching.

We do not sell your personal data.

5. Local Storage

Some information may be stored locally on your device, including:

  • Installation ID
  • Onboarding completion state
  • Language preference
  • Certain temporary or history-related app data

This local storage is used to maintain the app experience and support core functionality.

6. Information Sent to Servers

The following information may be sent to servers in order to provide the core functionality of the app:

  • Barcode numbers
  • Ingredient text entered manually or extracted via OCR
  • Selected dietary or preference settings
  • Product information and analysis request data
  • Installation ID or similar technical usage keys
  • Language code and plan context

These transfers are necessary to generate analysis results, resolve or identify products, and manage usage and subscription status.

7. Data Retention

We may retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our policies.

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data, operational needs, and legal requirements.

8. Children's Privacy

SnaxAI - Check Foods for Diet is not designed for children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe that a child's data has been provided to us, please contact us.

9. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

10. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have the right to:

  • Request information about what data is processed
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to certain processing activities
  • Request data portability where applicable

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the contact details below.

  • RevenueCat
  • Google Play / Apple App Store purchase infrastructure
  • OCR and text recognition services
  • External product or ingredient data sources

These third-party services may process data according to their own privacy policies.
