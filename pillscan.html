# Privacy Policy **Last updated: January 2025** Pill Scan ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Pill Scan mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our App. ## 1. Information We Collect ### 1.1 Account Information - Email address (for account creation and authentication) - Authentication data from Google or Apple Sign-In (if used) ### 1.2 Medicine Data - Medicine names, dosage forms, and quantities - Expiration dates - Barcodes (when scanned) - Custom labels and notes - Medication schedules and adherence records ### 1.3 Technical Data - Device type and operating system - App usage analytics (anonymized) - Crash reports and error logs ## 2. How We Use Your Information We use your information to: - Provide and maintain the App's functionality - Send expiration alerts and medication reminders - Sync your data across devices - Improve App performance and user experience - Respond to support requests We **do not**: - Sell your personal data to third parties - Share your medicine data with advertisers - Use your health data for marketing purposes ## 3. Data Storage and Security ### 3.1 Cloud Storage Your data is stored securely in Supabase cloud infrastructure: - All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) - Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 encryption - Row-level security ensures you can only access your own data - Servers are located in secure data centers ### 3.2 Local Storage Some data is cached locally on your device for offline access: - Local data is stored in encrypted application storage - Data is cleared when you sign out ## 4. Data Sharing We may share your data only in the following circumstances: - **Service Providers**: With Supabase (cloud infrastructure) and Firebase (analytics/notifications) to operate the App - **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights - **With Your Consent**: If you explicitly agree to share data ## 5. Your Rights You have the right to: - **Access**: View all personal data we store about you - **Export**: Download your medicine data (PDF or CSV) - **Correction**: Update or correct your information - **Deletion**: Delete your account and all associated data - **Portability**: Receive your data in a portable format To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below or use the in-app data export feature. ## 6. Data Retention - Your data is retained as long as your account is active - Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days - Anonymized analytics may be retained for service improvement ## 7. Children's Privacy The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. ## 8. Third-Party Services The App uses the following third-party services: - **Supabase**: Database and authentication (see [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)) - **Google Sign-In**: Authentication (see [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)) - **Apple Sign-In**: Authentication (see [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)) - **Firebase**: Analytics and push notifications (see [Firebase Privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)) ## 9. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. ## 10.
